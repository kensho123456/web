Notes

Everybody's different, of course, but it's fair to say that any kind of hearing loss takes a wee bit of getting used to, so I hope these notes help you get there quickly. The real difficulty in my opinion is with getting other people's understanding and I hope this site helps in that regard also. The fact is that we have to be a wee bit more mindful of our safety than before and it would be foolish not to recognise this. Once upon a time cyclists were banned from going on the pavement but not anymore and now I have to worry about cyclists coming up behind me and hitting either me or the dog. It was easier when cyclists kept to the road and we can't even be sure of a taxi anymore in case the driver is of the muslim faith and not allow in dogs whether they are Assistance Dogs or not. Please call back as I update the site.
